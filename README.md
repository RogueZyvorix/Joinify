# Joinify

## Setup

### How to Use (Important!)

1. **Setup Mod:** Install the Joinify mod and [E4mc](https://modrinth.com/mod/e4mc) on your Minecraft client.

2. Press the **Joinify Social** button on the right side of your screen.
<details>
  <summary>see picture</summary>
  <img src="https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/0fe9b974897ca25327eb0cf7271ebcad6411c186.jpeg" alt="foto 1">
</details>

3. Enter the username of the player you want to add as a friend.
<details>
  <summary>see picture</summary>
  <img src="https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/25c3488aeb09f999c807e9fb24633214dbc2a783.jpeg" alt="foto 2">
</details>

4. Open your world and click **Open to LAN** (you set it first).
<details>
  <summary>see picture</summary>
  <img src="https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/237446716ff3bdffc0cfad832b671fad4dbd678f.jpeg" alt="foto 3">
</details>

5. Return to the pause menu and click **Joinify Social**.
<details>
  <summary>see picture</summary>
  <img src="https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/769fad032687d5bd4121c65aa88f636544dbdd9f.jpeg" alt="foto 5">
</details>

6. Send invites to your online friends and start playing!
<details>
  <summary>see picture</summary>
  <img src="https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/39842bef4b473b8faee72910d12f9d17167e667e.jpeg" alt="foto 6">
</details>
